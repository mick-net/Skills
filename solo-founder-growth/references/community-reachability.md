# Community Reachability

Use this for `still-building/prelaunch`, `launched/no revenue`, and `$0-$1k MRR` work. A buyer persona is not enough. The founder needs a reachable community or channel where real prospects already gather.

## Gate

Before treating an idea as build-ready, answer:

1. Which community or channel contains the buyer?
2. Is the founder genuinely connected to it, or only observing from outside?
3. Where do members gather: X, Reddit, Discord, Slack, forums, LinkedIn, newsletters, marketplaces, events, app stores, search queries, or existing customer lists?
4. What repeated complaint, workaround, job post, support thread, search query, or paid behavior proves the pain?
5. Can the founder name or find 10 reachable people/accounts this week?

If the answer is no, the next action is community/customer research, not product scope.

## What Counts As Proof

- Repeated complaints from the community.
- Public posts asking for solutions.
- People already paying for worse alternatives.
- Manual workarounds, spreadsheets, scripts, agencies, consultants, or internal tools.
- Direct replies, calls, pilots, preorders, or paid manual work.

## Optional Deeper Skills

Use these only for prelaunch or `$0-$1k MRR` problems:

- `find-community`: when the founder does not know which community/channel to start from.
- `validate-idea`: when the idea needs sell-before-build validation.
- `mvp`: when scope must shrink to manual, no-code, or weekend-sized delivery.
- `first-customers`: when the next bottleneck is one-by-one sales.

Do not load these for `$10k+ MRR` work unless the product is entering a new market from scratch.

If these skills are missing, continue with this reference. For source/install pointers, see `optional-skill-sources.md`.

## Output

Use:

- `Reachable Community`: where the buyer gathers.
- `Founder Connection`: genuine member, adjacent, outsider, or unknown.
- `Repeated Pain`: evidence from complaints, workarounds, search, spending, or calls.
- `10 Prospects`: concrete people/accounts or where to find them.
- `Next Action`: one outreach, research, or public proof action this week.
- `Build Decision`: build, validate first, narrow community, or drop.
